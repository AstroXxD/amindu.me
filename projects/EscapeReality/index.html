Welcome to our fun roleplay server! It's made using FiveM's qbcore framework and developed by me, Flash Killer and Yuki. Our server is all about customizing your experience. You can create your own clothes, explore unique places (Custom MLO), take on special jobs with whitelist access, and even get involved in exciting illegal activities. Not to mention, we have cool custom cars too

Whether you're new to roleplaying or a pro, everyone is welcome to join our server. It's a place where you can have loads of fun, let your imagination run wild, and go on incredible adventures. Come be a part of our amazing project and see what roleplay gaming is all about!
